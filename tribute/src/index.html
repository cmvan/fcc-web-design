TWICE

South Korea's National Girl Group

Ever since they were formed from the 2015 reality show Sixteen by JYP Entertainment, TWICE (Korean: 트와이스 / Japanese: トゥワイス) have been leaving a lasting impression on the KPop industry with their bright, bubbly concepts and addictive discography. Made up of members Im Nayeon, Yoo Jeongyeon, Hirai Momo, Minatozaki Sana, Park Jihyo, Myoui Mina, Kim Dahyun, Son Chaeyoung, and Chou Tzuyu, the nonet have excelled in the industry for their ability to sell millions of albums, give phenomenal performances, and woo KPop fans across the globe with their compassionate and hilarious personalities.
